Jesus Christ is the Son of God, the second person of the Holy Trinity, and the Savior of humanity. He was born in Bethlehem, lived a sinless life, performed miracles, taught about love and forgiveness, and ultimately sacrificed Himself for the sins of the world. His resurrection from the dead is the cornerstone of Christian faith, symbolizing victory over sin and death.
